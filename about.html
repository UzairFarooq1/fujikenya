WELCOME TO FUJI KENYA

Innovative Healthcare Solutions Provider

Fuji Kenya Ltd (FKL) was formed in 1981 and has been successfully representing Fuji film Japan since inception. Fuji film, Japan is the pioneer in diagnostic imaging and information system for healthcare facilities. They have clinically proven products and technologies, which are constantly evolving to help medical professionals perform more effectively and efficiently.

FKL’s main mission has been to incorporate latest digital technology in the Medical field and provide the best quality products along with a strong Service backup. Fuji Kenya’s service has been its focal point- a fact easily proven by their records of accomplishment on repairs and the number of service contracts they are presently working with.

All equipment is maintained by well qualified and trained Biomedical Engineers. Most of the working staff have a technical background and have had an average of 10-15 years experience in the medical industry. To evolve the quality of service provided to the customer and to ensure zenith success the Biomedical Engineers are trained constantly at Fujifilm’s factory training centers to obtain firsthand operational and technical training in the area of their expertise

As installations grew in East Africa, Fuji Kenya opened sales offices in Uganda and Tanzania in order to provide the best services to the clients. The future looks bright with the Fujifilm brand getting stronger with Fuji Kenya’s continuous efforts and the strong endorsement from existing clients.
